1. Power on the computer and repeatedly press the F8 key until the screen
 titled "Advanced Boot Options" show up on the screen.
 2. Select the option "Repair Your Computer" using the up and down arrow keys and press Enter
 3. Under "System Recovery Options", you can change a keyboard input method. Click on NEXT
 4. Under User name, Select your User Account. Enter the password (Optional), click on OK.
 5. Click on "Reinstall Windows"
 6. Please DO NOT POWER OFF THE SYSTEM during re-installation process.  The Out-Of-Box-Experience (OOBE) pop-up
 windows should appear once the process is complete. At which point you will be able to register/set-up the 
 system once again.
